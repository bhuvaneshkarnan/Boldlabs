PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us when you book a discovery call, send an email, or subscribe to our monthly packages. This includes your name, email address, phone number, company name, and details about your project or business operations.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to communicate with you, process your service requests, design and implement your website or automation workflows, send booking confirmations and reminders, and deliver monthly reports.

3. DATA SECURITY & RETENTION

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We may integrate third-party APIs and services (such as Cal.com, Calendly, Stripe, Twilio, and CRM tools) into your project. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them to understand how they handle your data.

5. YOUR RIGHTS & CONTACT

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data stored by us. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: hello@boldlabs.studio.
